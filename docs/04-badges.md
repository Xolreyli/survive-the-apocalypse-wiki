# Badges

Badges are awarded for thresholds or accomplishments.
Players can show badges **above their heads**.

## Survival milestones
- Survive **5 Days**
- Survive **10 Days**
- Survive **25 Days**
- Survive **50 Days**
- Survive **100 Days**
- Survive **250 Days**

## Generator / Fueler series
- **Fueler I:** Reach Generator Level 3
- **Fueler II:** Reach Generator Level 4
- **Fueler III:** Reach Generator Level 5
- **Fueler IV:** Reach Generator Level 6

## Slayer series (kills in one round)
- **Slayer I:** Kill 200 Zombies
- **Slayer II:** Kill 1,000 Zombies
- **Slayer III:** Kill 5,000 Zombies

## Other badges
- **Crafting Master:** Reach Crafting Level 4
- **Savior:** Revive a teammate
- **Masked Up:** Obtain a Gas Mask
- **Giantslayer:** Slay a Brute
- **Restoring Power:** Restore all Powerplants
- **Signal Detected:** Repair the Radio Tower
- **Escape:** Escape the city
- **Soloist:** Escape the city without a team
