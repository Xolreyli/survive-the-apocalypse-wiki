## Types

- **Starter zombie:** Slow and the least amount of health.
- **Runner:** A little quicker than the starter zombie and low health. 
- **Shield:** Riot police type of zombie. Has a riot shield and baton. Does a decent amount of damage.
- **Muscle:** Orange and massive. High damage. Lunges and throws rocks.
- **Screamer:** White with long claws. Extremely fast. Screams (red aura) then charges.
- **Brute:** Massive, high damage, very high health. (Also appears as Blood Moon boss.)
- **Spitter:** Neon green. Spits acid (acid deals damage).
- **Phaser:** Ghost zombie that phases through defenses (gates, barbed wire, fences, etc).
- **Bio-Hazard Zombie:** Hazmat suit runner. Can drop **Gas Masks** to enter the Irradiated Zone.
- **Exploder:** Dark green, slow, explodes when health hits 0. Explosion harms players and zombies.

## Crates (loot boxes)
Light brown boxes that spawn around the map and provide loot.
Glow color indicates loot rarity (**green is most common**).

Possible loot:
- weapons
- ammo
- bandages / medkits
- backpacks
