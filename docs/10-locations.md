## Hospital
- Marked with a **Red H**
- Better loot inside
- Zombies guard outside and inside
- Rain resets crates and zombies

## Police Station
- Marked with a **Police Badge**
- Better loot inside
- Zombies guard outside and inside
- Rain resets crates and zombies

## Bandit Post
- Outpost with hostile non-zombie NPCs
- Highlighted **orange** on the map
- Contains crates
- Contains the **Radio Part** required to fix the Radio Tower for extraction

## Nuclear Powerplant (Reactor dungeon)
Located in the **Irradiated Zone** (green highlighted area on map).
- Map icon is **purple** once discovered
- Dungeon-style, timed mission
- Waves of zombies + final boss
- Basement opens on completion with loot chests
  - Chest glow indicates rarity (**green common â†’ yellow rare**)
- Contains battery packs, ammo boxes, and reactor parts (worth lots of scrap)
- One special crate at the bottom:
  - only **one person** can open it
  - **global** loot (not per-player)
  - contains either **Ray Gun** or **Flamethrower**

## Power plants
Yellow map objects spread around the map.
- Repairing them increases your day multiplier (+1x each)
- **All powerplants repaired** is required for extraction

## Irradiated Zone
Green highlighted map area.
- Requires **Gas Mask** (dropped by Bio-Hazard zombies) to access safely (as implied by the source)
