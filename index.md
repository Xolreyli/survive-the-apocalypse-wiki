# Survive the Apocalypse Wiki

**Survive the Apocalypse** is a zombie survival game where players **build and fortify bases**, **fight zombies**, **loot**, and **kill bosses** across increasingly difficult **waves**.

You can attempt **Extraction on Day 100** (if you meet the requirements).

## Quick facts
- All players start with **a Bat + a Backpack**, even if a **Class** is equipped
- Progression ramps through **waves** that get harder over time
- Key systems: **Classes, Badges, Crafting Table (4 levels), Hunger, Blood Moons, Supply Crates, Powerplants, Nuclear Reactor dungeon**
